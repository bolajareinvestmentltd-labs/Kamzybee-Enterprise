Place optimized hero videos here:

- hero.webm (preferred, smaller and modern)
- hero.mp4 (fallback)

Encoding recommendations:
- WebM VP9 or AV1, 720p or 1080p, bitrate 1.5-4 Mbps
- MP4 H.264 baseline/profile, 720p, ~3 Mbps
- Keep duration short (6-12s) and loop-friendly

After adding files, run the app and verify the hero plays and falls back to `/hero-poster.svg` when autoplay is blocked.
